History

The ICC Cricket World Cup is the international championship of One Day International (ODI) cricket. It is organized by the International Cricket Council (ICC), which was first held in 1975.

Participating Teams

  • India
  • Australia
  • England
  • New Zealand
  • Pakistan
  • South Africa
  • West Indies
  • Sri Lanka
  • Afghanistan
  • Bangladesh

Schedule

The tournament usually takes place every 4 years with matches held across various venues.

Past Winners

  • 2023 - Australia
  • 2019 - England
  • 2015 - Australia
  • 2011 - India
