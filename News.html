Shubman Gill 'Unwell' Days After Asia Cup Squad Announcement

23 August 2025 | CricVilla News Desk

Shubman Gill has been in focus for the last couple of months. He was named the Indian cricket team's Test captain and excelled in England. Then, there were doubts over his selection for the Asia Cup T20, but he made it through and was named the vice-captain.

In the midst of this, a report has claimed that the 25-year-old Indian cricket team Test captain is likely to miss a major BCCI event, which would have been the top Indian cricket team stars' first event since the England tour.

While no official update has been provided by the BCCI yet, fans and experts are waiting eagerly for clarity. Gill’s presence in the Asia Cup would be vital for India’s campaign, given his form and leadership role in recent months.
