Overview

The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India, established in 2008. It is one of the most popular and financially successful cricket leagues worldwide.

Teams

  • Chennai Super Kings
  • Mumbai Indians
  • Royal Challengers Bangalore
  • Kolkata Knight Riders
  • Delhi Capitals
  • Sunrisers Hyderabad

Schedule

The IPL season is typically held annually between March and May, featuring multiple matches played across different Indian cities.

Past Winners
