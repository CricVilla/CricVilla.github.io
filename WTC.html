Overview

The ICC World Test Championship (WTC) is the international championship of Test cricket, introduced by the International Cricket Council in 2019 to bring context and a championship structure to Test matches.

Participating Teams

  • India
  • Australia
  • England
  • New Zealand
  • Pakistan
  • South Africa
  • West Indies
  • Sri Lanka
  • Bangladesh
  • Zimbabwe

Format

The championship runs over a two-year cycle where teams accumulate points through Test series, culminating in a final to decide the champion.

Schedule

The WTC schedule is integrated with bilateral Test series and concludes with a final match between the two top teams.

Current Champions

As of 2025, South Africa are the current ICC World Test Champions.
