T20 World Cup

History

The ICC T20 World Cup is the international championship of Twenty20 cricket. It started in 2007 and is organized by the International Cricket Council (ICC).

Participating Teams

  • India
  • Australia
  • England
  • New Zealand
  • Pakistan
  • South Africa

Schedule

The tournament usually occurs every two years with matches held worldwide.

Past Winners

  • 2024 - India
  • 2022 - England
  • 2016 - West Indies
  • 2014 - Sri Lanka
