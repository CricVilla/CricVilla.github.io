Overview

The ICC Champions Trophy was a limited-overs cricket tournament organized by the International Cricket Council. It was considered the second most important ODI tournament after the World Cup.

History

The tournament started in 1998 and was last held in 2025. It featured the top cricketing nations competing in a condensed ODI format.

Participating Teams

  • Australia
  • England
  • New Zealand
  • Pakistan
  • South Africa

Past Winners

  • 2025 - India
  • 2017 - Pakistan
  • 2013 - India
  • 2009 - Australia
